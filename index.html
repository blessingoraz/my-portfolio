My name is Blessing Orazulume

Hi there! I’m Blessing, a Full-stack Software Developer. After getting a degree in Computer Science and also teaching Computer Studies to high school students, I decided to apply my love for technology and creative thinking towards becoming a software developer. I design and build web and mobile applications using Javascript. I currently work at Andela.

I love coming up with innovative ideas, implementing them, and seeing my vision work. Some of the projects I’ve worked on include:

  • Developing and implementing tools needed to automate processes for my team(a technology start-up that provides faster and better broadband) using React, Redux, MySQL and Node.
  • Building a CLI tool to aid my team with continuous deployment to development, staging and production environments.
  • A pizza application using stripe API for payments and Node Js on the server-side to create CRUD restful API.

When I’m not on the job, I love singing so you will mostly find me at Karaoke spots, dancing, drawing and going to adventurous places.

If you want to get in touch, you can message me on Twitter and LinkedIn. I also love to write technical blogs, you can find them on Medium and my personal blogs.
